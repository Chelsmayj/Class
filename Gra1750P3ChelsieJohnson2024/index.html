Crunchy Banana Nut Delight

This banana bread recipe creates a soft, moist, and flavorful loaf that’s perfect for any occasion. It combines ripe, mashed bananas with simple pantry ingredients like flour, sugar, eggs, and butter, resulting in a naturally sweet and comforting treat. A hint of vanilla and cinnamon enhances the flavor, while optional mix-ins like walnuts or chocolate chips can add texture and richness. The bread bakes to a golden brown crust, staying tender and delicious for days. Ideal for breakfast, a snack, or dessert, this easy-to-make banana bread is a crowd-pleaser every time.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups of Flour
  • 1 ½ tsp baking soda
  • ¼ tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 ½ Cup of Mashed Ripe Banana (3-4 Bananas)
  • 1 Cup of Sugar
  • ½ Cup of Vegetable Oil
  • 2 tbsp Milk
  • 1 Egg
  • ½ Cup of Chopped Walnuts
  • ⅓ Cup of Pecans

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. Grease a loaf pan and dust with flour.
  3. Whisk the flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt until combined.
  4. In another bowl, mix the egg, sugar, and vegetable oil until combined.
  5. Add the dry mixture to the egg mixture and stir until blended.
  6. Add in chopped walnuts and pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
  7. Top with remaining pecans.
  8. Bake for 60-80 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
